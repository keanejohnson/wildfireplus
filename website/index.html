Millions of acres burn annually in wildfires across the globe. As population grows and spreads to all lands, as climate change challenges the environments, and as wildfires grows in intensity and frequency, every second counts.

HOTzone is an attempt to predict the probability of the spread of an existing fire, to provide an early warning.
